Value vs. Growth: High Price/Book (P/B > 3) → Growth stock. Low P/B (< 2) → Value stock. Fama-French predicts value stocks earn higher returns over time.

7Check recent factor performance.

Type FAMA GO or search "Fama French" on Bloomberg. Alternatively, type EQS GO and create screens for small-cap value vs. large-cap growth stocks.

Based on recent data, are the size and value premiums still positive?

Part 4: Market Efficiency Test

8Event study: Earnings announcement reaction.

Pick any stock. Type AAPL Equity ERN GO to see recent earnings announcements.

Find the most recent earnings surprise (actual vs. estimate). Then type GP GO and zoom in to the announcement date.

FieldYour Finding
Stock ticker
Earnings date
EPS estimate vs. actual
Stock price change on announcement day
Price change over next 5 days

9Assess market efficiency.

Based on your event study:

  • Did the price adjust quickly (within the announcement day)?
  • Was there post-announcement drift (continued movement in the same direction)?
  • Which form of EMH does your evidence support or challenge?
Reminder: One data point does not prove or disprove EMH. Professional research uses hundreds of events and statistical tests. This exercise is designed to familiarize you with the data and the logic, not to reach definitive conclusions.
