Part 3: Corporate Bond Analysis

3Find a corporate bond using Bloomberg search.

Type SRCH GO (Bond Search). Set filters:

  • Currency: USD
  • Sector: Corporate
  • Maturity: 5–10 years
  • Rating: Investment Grade (BBB or higher)

Select one bond and type its CUSIP or ticker followed by Corp, then YAS GO.
